St. Louis, MO

This is one of my favorite cities to visit. I enjoy looking at the old buildings.

Omaha, NE

Omaha is beatiful. I go here for the parks & recreation.

Denver, CO

Denver has a lot of fun things to do. Musuems, and scenic areas.

Detroit, MI

Detroit is the car capital of the world. While here on a business trip I visited the Henry Ford museum.

Wichita, KS

Wichita has an old west museum and a rich native american history.

Keystone, SD

One of the more difficult drives that i've taken but definitely worth the visit. While here we visited Bear Country USA, and Mount Rushmore.
